# besterds

> Ever wondered where you would stand without those nasty cheaters?  
> **Besterds shows you.**

---

## What is besterds?

Besterds is a browser extension for Codeforces that adds a cleaner, alternative leaderboard by hiding or highlighting suspicious users.

```text
The idea is simple — even my 5-year-old younger brother understood it:

Original Codeforces standings
↓
Besterds filtering
↓
Pure leaderboard

⚠️ Besterds can make mistakes.
It is not an official Codeforces verdict and should not be treated as final proof of cheating.
